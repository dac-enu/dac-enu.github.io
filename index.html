Welcome!

The purpose of this website is to raise awareness surrounding web browser privacy and security. It details what are the best web browsers to mitigate tracking on websites as well as any telemetry that they send to developers or any other third parties. The site will also allow you to test your web browser for JavaScript-based tracking methods and will demonstrate a method of creating a unique fingerprint value based on the reported values that your web browser reports.
